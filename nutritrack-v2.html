How to export from Whoop
1. Open the Whoop app
2. Tap More (bottom right)
3. App Settings then Data Export
4. Tap Create Export
5. Download the zip from the email Whoop sends (within ~1 hour)
6. Unzip it — you'll get four CSV files
Import workouts.csv for activities · Import physiological_cycles.csv for recovery, HRV and sleep
Note: Whoop does not export steps or VO2Max — those metrics are not in the CSV files

How to export from Apple Health
1. Open the Health app on iPhone
2. Tap your profile picture (top right)
3. Scroll down, tap Export All Health Data
4. Share the zip to Files or your Mac
5. Unzip it and upload export.xml here
